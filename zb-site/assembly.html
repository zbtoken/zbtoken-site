THE ASSEMBLY

// community governance framework :: by the holders, for the holders //
// PROTOCOL_DOCTRINE //

ZB Assembly is the community governance framework of ZB Token. Inspired by Zenon's Accelerator-Z, it allows holders to propose, discuss, and vote on initiatives that shape the future of the protocol.

The framework is intentionally minimal at launch. As the community grows and matures, governance evolves with it — from transparent founder-led decisions, to multi-signature trust networks, to community consensus through structured discussion and signed votes.

When the project is fully delivered, the community holds everything. The future belongs to those who hold the token, build the dApps, and play the game.

[ ASSEMBLY_TREASURY ]

// 7,506,796 ZB :: 35.75% OF TOTAL SUPPLY //

The Assembly Treasury holds 7,506,796 ZB — the largest single allocation of the protocol. It is the all-in-one community fund that powers everything beyond the game economy and liquidity reserves.

What it funds:

→ Development (engaging contributors, paying for technical work)
→ Community programs (airdrops, ARG, contests, ambassador rewards)
→ Marketing & partnerships
→ Bug bounties, audits, translations, content creation
→ Anything that advances the protocol

How it operates:

→ Today — founder-led, transparent, every transaction on-chain
→ Soon — multi-signature with trusted long-term contributors
→ Eventually — community consensus, structured proposals, signed votes

Every transaction is verifiable. Every payment leaves an on-chain trace. The wallet address will be published once the protocol enters its public launch phase.

ZB does not depend on external validation. The protocol functions and grows independently. The Assembly is funded by ZB itself, governed by ZB holders, and answers to no one outside the community.

Treasury funds are deployed for verifiable deliverables — game development, security audits, community programs. Not for hype, not for marketing pumps.

[ GOVERNANCE_EVOLUTION ]

PHASE_01

FOUNDER-LED // TRANSPARENT

The founder makes operational decisions according to publicly stated rules. All wallet activity is on-chain and verifiable. Community provides feedback through the Signal channels. This is the bootstrap phase — necessary, transparent, and intentionally temporary.

PHASE_02

MULTI-SIGNATURE // TRUSTED

Treasury wallets transition to multi-signature control (3-of-5 or similar). Trusted long-term contributors share signing authority. Decisions require consensus among signers. Slower, but vastly more secure. This happens when the right contributors emerge — not on a fixed timeline.

PHASE_03

COMMUNITY CONSENSUS

Once the community is established and engaged, governance becomes structured. Proposals are submitted on the forum, discussed openly, and voted on through cryptographic signatures (off-chain but verifiable). The Assembly executes decisions transparently. No complex smart contracts, no expensive infrastructure — just clear process, verifiable signatures, and on-chain transactions for execution. This evolves naturally — when the community is ready, not before.
